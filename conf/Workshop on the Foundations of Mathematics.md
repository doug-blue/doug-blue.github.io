# Workshop on the Foundations of Mathematics

February 27-28, 2027

[The Center for Philosophy of Science](https://www.centerphilsci.pitt.edu)


**Description**: Questions about the nature of mathematics have long inspired both mathematical innovation and philosophical reflection. This conference brings together early-career researchers in mathematics and philosophy to discuss issues surrounding mathematical truth, computability, proof, infinity, and mathematical knowledge. Through a series of invited lectures, participants will engage with both technical developments and conceptual challenges, contributing to a tradition of inquiry that lies at the heart of the foundations of mathematics.
